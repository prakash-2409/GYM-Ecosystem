# GymOS Demo Script

## Before the meeting (5 minutes setup)

1. Open `/dashboard/settings`
2. Change gym name to prospect's gym name
3. Change tagline if known
4. Pick their brand color if known
5. Click **Save Changes**
6. Open `/kiosk` in new tab — verify name shows
7. Open `/member-app` — verify name shows
8. You are ready

---

## Demo flow (10-12 minutes)

### Step 1 — Kiosk (2 min)

- Open `/kiosk` fullscreen (F11)
- Type `1042` → show Rahul Kumar
- Point out: name, plan, attendance, fee status
- Type `2031` → show fee warning (orange banner)
- Say: _"Receptionist sees this immediately"_
- Type `9999` → show error state

### Step 2 — Admin Dashboard (4 min)

- Navigate to `/dashboard` via Demo Nav
- Show stat cards → revenue, members, checkins
- Click Members → show list, search "priya"
- Click Priya's row → show full profile
- Show tabs: attendance calendar, progress chart
- Go to Fees → show Mark Collected button
- Go to Notifications → show bulk WhatsApp compose

### Step 3 — Plan Scheduler (2 min)

- Go to `/dashboard/plans/library`
- Show template cards
- Click Assign → show bulk member selection
- Click Preview PDF → show generated PDF
- Say: _"Coach assigns to 50 members in 2 minutes, everyone gets WhatsApp with PDF automatically"_

### Step 4 — Member App (2 min)

- Open `/member-app` on your phone (or show on laptop in mobile viewport)
- Show check-in QR scan flow
- Show My Plan section
- Show Progress chart
- Say: _"Member installs this from WhatsApp link. No Play Store needed, works on any phone"_

### Step 5 — Close (2 min)

- Go to `/dashboard/settings`
- Show how to change gym name/logo/color
- Say: _"This is YOUR gym's system, not shared with any other gym. Completely yours."_
- Then close.

## Objection responses

### "We already use JetFitness"

> "JetFitness is the same app for 600 gyms. This is built only for your gym. Your name, your branding, your way."

### "Too expensive"

> "One member who pays because of auto reminder covers the full month cost. First 30 days completely free anyway."

### "We will think about it"

> "I understand. Let me set it up with your gym name right now and leave it running for 30 days free. No payment needed today. If you don't like it just say so."
