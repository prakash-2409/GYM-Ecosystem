# GymOS Project Roadmap

Welcome to the GymOS Roadmap! This document outlines the planned milestones and features for the GymOS platform. We are actively seeking contributions during **GirlScript Summer of Code (GSSoC)** to achieve these goals.

## 🎯 Phase 1: Core Platform Foundation (Completed)
- ✅ **Backend API**: Node.js/Express structure, JWT auth, RBAC.
- ✅ **Web Admin Dashboard**: Attendance tracking, Fee management, Member profiling.
- ✅ **Mobile App (Flutter)**: PWA & Native app for members with QR check-in.
- ✅ **Notification Engine**: WhatsApp (WATI), SMS (MSG91), Push Notifications (FCM).

## 🚀 Phase 2: Monetization & Extensibility (Current / GSSoC Focus)
- 🔲 **Payment Gateway Integration**: Integrate Razorpay to collect subscription fees online.
- 🔲 **Asset Storage Integration**: Implement Cloudflare R2 / AWS S3 for profile pictures and logo uploads.
- 🔲 **Advanced Workout & Diet Tracker UI**: Polish the web interfaces for assigning personalized routines.
- 🔲 **Superadmin Dashboard**: Centralized portal for onboarding new gyms, managing global billing, and analytics.
- 🔲 **Unit & Integration Testing**: Achieve 80% test coverage across critical backend routes using Jest.

## 🔮 Phase 3: Analytics & AI (Future)
- 🔲 **AI-Powered Insights**: Predict member churn based on attendance drops.
- 🔲 **Advanced WhatsApp Chatbots**: Allow members to check their fee status via WhatsApp conversational flows.
- 🔲 **Multi-Branch Support**: For franchises with multiple physical locations under one brand.
- 🔲 **Hardware Integration**: Support for Biometric fingerprint scanners and RFID cards (IoT).

## 💡 Proposing Features
Have a great idea? Please open a **Feature Request** issue! We are always open to community suggestions that align with our vision of empowering Indian gym owners.
